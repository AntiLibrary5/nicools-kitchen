## Ingredients

* 1 kilo of loubieh 
* 2 medium white onions
* Salt 
* 7 spices
* Unlimited quantity of good quality Olive Oil


## Instructions

1. Ask your mom, or dad, or grandma (because we both know that your grandpa didn't even bother cooking) for the recipe because I did it a long time ago and forgot the steps
2. But just in case you're still here, because you really love my blog, then here's some simple steps for you, girl 
3. Remove the stamp for the loubiyeh and cut it in half if too long (I usually get frozen loubiyeh) 
4. Dice your onions
5. In a pan, put a generous amout of olive oil, and then throw in the loubiyeh and the onions
6. Add the salt and the 7 spices and start cooking on low heat until tender and cooked 
7. It will take a while (around 2 hours to fully cook) and you might need to add more oil as you go
8. Short and easy recipe, but please don't take my word for it
