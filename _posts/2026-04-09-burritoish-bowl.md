Apparently a day in Lama's life is really cool and comforting. Since my "deadly" car crash (I like to say deadly to sound dramatic), I don't have a car. Lama was kind enough to chauffeur me to Beirut and have me stay over to live a day (dayS...) in her life. Turns out her day consists of eating and thinking what to eat next, which I don't mind at all. Lama if you ever read this, please invite me to days in your life again. 

## Ingredients

* 1 kg of chicken breasts
* 1 can corn 
* 1 avocado
* 1 big tomato
* 1 onion
* 1 cup of "my smile" rice 
* 150g of greek yogurt
* 1 tablespoon mayo 
* 1 tablespoon ketchup
* 1 tablespoon red vinegar
* Salt 
* Black Pepper
* Butter 
* Onion and Garlic Powder 
* Paprika 
* chilli powder
* Cajun Seasoning


## Instructions

1. Have Lama , an no other, come pick you up and drive you to Beirut
2. Once in Beirut, head to the supermakert and get the above list of ingredients, and have Lama tell the guy to cut the chicken into strips
3. Once back home, marinate your chicken with salt, black pepper, chilli powder, paprika, and cajun seasoning and let it sit for 30 seconds (we were hungry)
4. All the below steps are to be done in parallel, so have your Lama help you
5. Heat up some oil, and cook your chicken until cooked
6. In another pan, cook up the corn with some salt and black pepper and butter, ekleh! 
7. Wash your "my smile" rice until water runs clear and cook it, plain, with a bit of salt
8. While Lama is doing steps 5, 6, and 7, go watch AL Jazeera, because gurl, you deserve it, it's tiring to be chauffeured around. 
9. For the Guac, there's no guac hehehehe 
10. Mash the avocado with a bit of salt and black pepper, and mix mix mix mix mix mix mix mix mix mix 
11. For the pico de gallo (prounouced gayo, you illetrate human being), dice the tomato and the onion, mix it with salt, black pepper, the vinegar, and ketchup, then go thanks Lama's mom for what used to be her secret ingrdient
12. Lama called this ranch sauce, but I prefer to call it ranch sauce: mix the greek yo, the ma yo, the onion powder, the garlic powder, the salt, together, and that's it 
13. Now plate everything, no sorry, have Lama plate everything, because she likes law and order 
14. Sit and eat
