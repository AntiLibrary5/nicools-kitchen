## Ingredients

* 1 big fat lebanese potato
* 2 thick cut beef fillet steaks from your local butcher
* Salt 
* Black Pepper 
* Knob or 2 knobs of butter
* 1 big clove of garlic
* A bit of oil (any kind)


## Instructions

### For the Fries
1. As the saying goes, fries for the beautiful eyes 
2. Call your dealer, then get the pealer, and you know the rest
3. Cut the potato into thick fries and soak them in cold water for a few minutes 
4. Boil some water and cook your fries for 8 minutes 
5. After 8 miutes, remove the fries from the water and pat them dry, as dry as your chats
6. Heat up the oil, and cook the fries until crispy, or as thy say in Tripoli; crespy, and golden. 
7. Salt the fries and close your eyes

### For the steak
1. Head to Japan, and get yourself a regular fillet cut from any Halal lebanese butcher you find there 
2. Pat dry your fillet, as fry as the fries that were pat dried drier than your chats 
3. Get a good skillet (I have a really bad one that is probably cancerogenic), put a bit of oil and heat the hell out of it
4. Please don't cook the steak unless your pan is very hot, just like you are girl 
5. Black Pepper and salt your fillet right before puting them over the heat, because we don't want the steak to throw away its moisture
6. Cook the steak on medium to high heat for 2-3 minutes on one side, and then once the crust is formed, flip to other side
7. Crush your garlic and throw it in the skillet alongside the butter and start bathing your steak with this liquid gold, for 2-3 minutes 
8. Remove the steak steak and let in rest for 5 minutes 
9. Cut the fillet into slices, plate your dish and enjoy
10. Girl, you thought I forgt about the juices, oh well I didn't 
11. Drizzle your steak juice on your steak, and enjoy 
12. Give yourself a hug, you just turned your local beef fillet steak into a fine dining piece of shit
