It was just two weeks ago that I realized that people either eat kafta w batata or kafta w rez. We, in our esteemed and established household, eat kafta w batata w rez and then we shame each other for gaining weight. But I wanted to change the course of history and decided to make Kafta w Batata w Rez, just like the good old times...weight? sorry I meant wait? Anw, enjoy this while I enjoy something else. 

## Ingredients

* 400g of finely minced meat
* Three quarter bouquet of ba2douniss
* 2 big white onions
* 4 medium potatoes (or potatos? I never know the difference)
* 1 red Pepper
* 1 chilli red Pepper
* 500 g of starined tomatoes (pomi or muti or the cheapest brand you can find)
* 2 tbsp pomegranate molasses
* 2 cups of water
* Oh live oil
* Salt
* Black Pepper
* Spice Spice Spice Spice Spice Spice Spice

## Instructions

### For the Kafta mix

1. Open instagram, I know the phone is in your hand, gurl 
2. Search for kolma3nikol and follow me if you don't 
3. Click on the link in the bio
4. Welcome to my blog, now scroll and try to find my kafta recipe, I named it "Don't eat that raw"
5. Read the recipe, enjoy it and follow the steps. 
6. Now don't turn the kafta into balls, or turn them into balls and then smash them to flatten them out. 
7. In a pan, heat up a drizzle of Oh may long live the oil, and brown your kafta from both sides, just like you do while tanning. 

### Now for kafta and batata

1. Close instagram, you've had enough
2. Cut the potatoes into thick slices or thin, or you know, just into slices, we don't label or shame here 
3. Heat up another pan (because you obvisouly didn't clean the one you used for the kafta), and tan the potatoes 
4. While your potatoes are browning, cut one big onion in half and then cut each half into slices. 
5. Now since your kafta and potatoes are cooked, now let the art begin
6. Call your favorite artist, or you can DM them @kolma3nikol, and ask them to come assemble the dish
7. Should go that way kafta potato onion, eat sleep repeat until you're out of all of them

### Now for the sauce and everything else

1. Please tell me you don't have an induction oven, because that would change the steps, and I'm not willing to
2. So on your good old oven, burn the red pepper and the chilli pepper by placing them directly on the flame 
3. Once they're completely burnt, remove them, put them in a bowl and close the bowl with w a cling film (this will help them sweat, and it will be easier to remove the skin)
4. Remove the skin and cut them into slices and replace them in the bowl
5. Add the pomegranate molasses 
6. Add the strained tomatoes
7. Add the water 
8. Add Salt 
9. Add Black Pepper 
10. Add me on instagram @kolma3nikol
11. Now blend everything together forever
12. POOR the sauce on your mozaic kafta dish
13. Place in the oven for maybe 40 minutes or something like that

### Now for the national assembly of Kafta rights

1. Remove from the oven 
2. Close the oven door 
3. Plate your plate 
4. Eat your plate 
5. Wash your plate 
6. Go take a break girl, have a tea, or a coffee or some water, because I bet you're dehydrated.
