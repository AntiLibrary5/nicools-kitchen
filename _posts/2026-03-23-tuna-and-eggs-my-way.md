Few years back, I faked being a Dr so that I can vote for my friend on something serious, because she's a Dr. And eversince, every now and then, I receive an email, with the subject: Dear Dr. Mansour. Mom, finally your sun is a Dr! To celebrate my new life as a Dr, I bought myself a 12$ tuna and turned it into a 1000000$ dish. 

## Ingredients

* 1 can Ortiz Tuna
* 1 big potato head
* Half a medium white onion 
* Handfull of green olives 
* 1 egg
* Salt and Black Pepper 
* 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar



## Instructions

1. Start by admiring your fancy tuna can, and picturing a dish that suits her
2. After weeks and weeks of her sitting in my cupboard, I finally decided what to do, so buckle up, because I'm taking you into a journey of something (not sure what that something is)
3. Slice your half onion and mix it with the red wine vinegar, and let it sit on the side, in a very confortable chair, especially if she has back pain
4. Get a head of potato, peal it, and cut it smol smol smol into cubes 
4. Heat up a pan, and start shalow frying the potato cubes
5. Thickly dice your green olives 
6. Once your potatoes are cooked, salt and black pepper them 
7. In the same pan, sear the fancy tuna from both sides
8. Then in the same pan, fry your egg
9. For the plating, put the potatoes in a flat dish, them throw in the olives, and then throw in the onions with a drizzle of their vinegar, top it with the seared tuna and finally your runny egg
10. Now enjoy please go fry this and try this at home, girl
