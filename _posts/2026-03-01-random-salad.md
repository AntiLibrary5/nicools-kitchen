For some people, what you see is what you get. Hello I'm some people. But not for this salad. What you see is a salad but what you get is a salad. 

## Ingredients

* 1 cup laban (not greek yogurt) 
* 1 avocado (not avokeydo)
* 1 tooth of garlic
* A bit of chilli flake for some kick
* Salt and Black Pepper
* A bit of Pap Rica
* 5 to 6 to 7 to 8 papers of lettuce
* 1 head of tomato 
* 1 pear (very optional but didn't want to throw it)
* 1 can of chickpeas
* 1 smalll red onion
* 1 whole Halloumi block of cheese straight from Cyprus

## Instructions

### For the dressing

1. Go back to the ingredients' list and blend the first 6 together 


### Now for salad

2. Starting with a second instruction because I'm always the first
3. Cut the lettuce into rustic chuncks 
4. Cut the tomato into cubes
5. Cut the pear into cubes 
6. Don't cut the onion into cubes 
7. Cut the onions into thin moons
8. Drain and wash the chickpeas and mix everything together with some salt and BP 
9. Slice your halloumi and grill the slices until caramelized 
10. Add the dressing to the salad (tbh not all of it, because DISCLAIMER: you just made a lot of dressing that you'll have to keep in the fridge but la flemme to calculate the exact amount needed)
11. Mix the salad (DO I seriously need to tell you that? isn't it the obvious next step to make?) 
12. Add your grilled halloumi and enjoy with a piece of toasted bread 
13. Now you tell me, what you see is actually what you get?
